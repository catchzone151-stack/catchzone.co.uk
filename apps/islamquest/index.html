About IslamQuest

IslamQuest is a fun and engaging Islamic learning app designed to help you grow your knowledge step-by-step. Study authentic Islamic content, test yourself with interactive quizzes, compete with friends and take part in global learning challenges — all in one place. Whether you're just starting or refreshing your knowledge, IslamQuest makes learning rewarding, social and enjoyable.

Authentic Islamic Knowledge

All lessons and quizzes in IslamQuest are based on authentic Islamic sources, carefully written for clarity and accuracy. Content is suitable for both children and adults and designed to be easy to understand — even for beginners.
