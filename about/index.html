About CatchZone

CatchZone is an independent app studio building thoughtful learning and productivity tools.

Our Mission

At CatchZone we design apps that help people learn, grow and stay engaged with meaningful content. Our goal is to build tools that are simple, privacy-respecting and genuinely useful in everyday life.

Current Projects

  • IslamQuest — Islamic learning through quests, quizzes and challenges.
  • Luna — A privacy-safe period tracker designed for teenagers.

Our Approach

  • Simple and intuitive
  • Respectful of user privacy
  • Designed for long-term learning and growth
